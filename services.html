Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How is financial coaching different from financial advising?

Financial advisors typically manage your investments for you. A financial coach educates and empowers you to make your own informed decisions — we focus on behavior, habits, and building financial literacy.

Do I need a minimum income to work with you?

Absolutely not! We work with people at all income levels. Our goal is to help you make the most of what you have, regardless of your starting point.

How long before I see results?

Most clients see meaningful progress within 30-60 days — whether that's a clear budget, a debt payoff plan, or their first investment. Major milestones like becoming debt-free depend on your starting position, but we set realistic timelines together.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your monthly plan at any time with 30 days' notice. We're confident you'll want to stay.

Is my financial data safe?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and never share your data with third parties. Your financial information is stored securely and is only visible to you and your assigned coach.
